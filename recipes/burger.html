Burger


Description:

Nothing says summer quite like a juicy grilled burger.
You'll find the full recipe below with step-by-step instructions.
Here's what you can expect when you make these top-rated burger patties:


Ingredients:

  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 teaspoons of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoons of black pepper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cups of fine bread crumbs
  • One burger bun

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill or pan on high heat, and add oil.
  2. Whisk together your egg salt and pepper in a bowl.
  3. Add ground beef and bread crubs to the mixture.
    Use your hands or a fork to mix until well blended.
  4. Form into 4 inch thick patties.
  5. Place your patties on the grill, cover, and cook 6-8 minutes per side or to desired doneness.
    (A thermometer placed in the center should read at least 160 degrees f.)
  6. Place on your burger bun and add any additional toppings.
  7. Enjoy!
